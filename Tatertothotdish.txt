Tater Tot Hotdish

1.5 lbs lean ground beef
1 onion
3/4 teaspoon salt
1 pinch black pepper
1 package (32 oz) tater tots, thawed
1 can (10.75 oz) condensed cream of mushroom soup
1 can (10.75 oz) condensed cream of celery soup
1 can (6 oz) French-fried onion rings


1. Preheat oven to 350 Fahrenheit.
2. Cook ground beef with onion, salt and pepper. Drain and spread into
bottom of a 9 by 13 inch baking dish. Add tater tots, mix soups together
and pour over dish. Top with onion rings.
3. Bake for 1 hr.
